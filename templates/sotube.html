About SoTube Team

The SoTube team is composed of six ambitious individuals who are dedicated to revolutionizing the world of online video advertising.
The name "SoTube" is derived from the combination of "So," which represents the last name of our Product Owner (PO),
and "Youtube," as the project was inspired by the vast potential of the popular video-sharing platform.
Our team consists of talented professionals from various disciplines, each playing a vital role in the success of SoTube.

Together, the SoTube team is committed to providing advertisers and YouTubers with a comprehensive and data-driven advertising platform.

Our Goals

  • Provide a comprehensive platform for advertisers and YouTubers to make informed decisions about ad placements.

  • Deliver a user-friendly and intuitive user interface for seamless interaction with the platform.
  • Continuously improve and innovate in the field of ML/AI to enhance the effectiveness and accuracy of our services.
  • Ensure reliable and efficient infrastructure through strong DevOps practices.
