About YAMP

At YAMP, we are dedicated to aiding our customers - Advertisers and YouTubers - in the decision-making process by providing a platform that visualizes and analyzes reactions to an ads video on a specific YouTube channel and the keywords related to that channel. Whether you are an advertiser looking to advertise products on YouTube channels, or a YouTuber looking to promote your channel to advertisers, YAMP is your go-to platform. We stand out from our competitors by offering unique features such as:

  • Identifying the types of products that are suitable for ads on channels.
  • Analyzing overall keywords that appear when searching for a YouTube channel.
  • Visualizing and analyzing reactions and evaluations of existing advertising videos on a specific YouTube channel.
